About Us

From a small home kitchen to Johannesburg's favourite artisan bakery.

How It All Began

Sweet Crumbs Bakery was started in 2015 by Amara Dlamini from her home kitchen in Melville, Johannesburg. It started as a weekend hobby where she would bake sourdough bread for her family and friends. People loved her bread so much that they started asking to buy it.

Word spread quickly through the neighbourhood and by 2016 Amara was baking every day just to keep up with orders. She made the decision to turn her passion into a full-time business and in 2017 she opened the first Sweet Crumbs store at 12 Baker Street in Melville.

The team grew from just Amara working alone to twelve full-time staff members. Today Sweet Crumbs serves hundreds of happy customers every week and delivers fresh baked goods across Johannesburg and greater Gauteng. Despite all the growth, the philosophy has stayed the same: use the best local ingredients, bake everything fresh daily and treat every customer like family.

Our Mission and Vision

Our Mission

To bake the freshest and most delicious artisan goods in Johannesburg and to bring real joy to every customer's table through quality, consistency and genuine care. We want every single person who orders from us to feel like they got something truly special.

Our Vision

To become South Africa's most loved artisan bakery brand - one that is trusted for its craft, respected for its commitment to sustainability and celebrated for the positive difference it makes in the local community.

Our Values

  • Quality without compromise - we never cut corners on ingredients
  • Community and inclusivity - good bread should be for everyone
  • Sustainability - we source locally and use compostable packaging
  • Creativity - every cake order is a chance to make something beautiful
  • Honesty - transparent pricing and no hidden charges ever

Meet Our Team

These are the talented people who bake, decorate and deliver every order with care.

Amara Dlamini

Founder and Head Baker

Amara has been baking for over 15 years and started Sweet Crumbs from her home kitchen. She leads the kitchen team and sets the standards for everything that leaves the bakery. Her sourdough recipe is still a closely guarded secret.

Sipho trained at a prestigious pastry school in Paris before coming back to South Africa. He is responsible for all our pastries, croissants and Danish items. He currently holds the record for folding the most croissant layers in one morning.

Lerato studied fine arts before discovering her passion for cake design. She turns every custom cake order into an edible work of art. Her designs have been featured in local wedding magazines and her waiting list is always full.

Our Journey

Here are some of the key moments that made Sweet Crumbs what it is today.

  1. 2015 - The First Loaf

    Sweet Crumbs started in Amara's home kitchen in Melville. The first sourdough loaves were sold at a local Saturday market. She sold out in 2 hours and had 30 pre-orders for the next weekend.

  2. 2017 - First Physical Store

    Sweet Crumbs moved into the current store at 12 Baker Street, Melville. The team grew to four full-time staff and delivery service launched across the surrounding suburbs.

  3. 2019 - Custom Cake Range

    The custom celebration cake range was launched and immediately became the fastest-growing part of the business. Lerato joined as the first dedicated cake designer.

  4. 2021 - Going Sustainable

    We switched to 100% compostable packaging and partnered with local urban farms so that all our flour is sourced within 100km of the bakery. Our carbon footprint dropped by over 40%.

  5. 2024 - Sandton City Kiosk

    A second location opened inside Sandton City Shopping Centre. The kiosk now serves over 200 customers per day on weekends and helped us reach a whole new set of customers in the northern suburbs.
